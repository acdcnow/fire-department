# Fire Department Info

Get real-time updates on fire department operations in Austria directly in Home Assistant.

**Key Features:**
* Monitor **Active Operations**, **Deployed Forces**, and **Completed Missions**.
* Data retrieved from state warning systems (WASTL).
* Rich attributes available for creating custom lists or tables in your dashboard.
* Fully configurable via the Home Assistant UI.
